Y O N

by Yanzhi Lai

A sculpture in itself, Yon is a multi-funtional object intended to be used as a furniture piece.

The unique form was designed with the concept of lightness and continuity. Through the visual process of tracing the curves, a unique view is offered from every angle. Yon employs the raw qualities of steel to create sculptural forms that are both beautiful and useful in the home.

Infos:

  • Materials: 1/2-inch Hot Rolled Steel Rod, Plexiglass
  • Techniques: MIG welding, Metal bending, Laser cutting
  • Year: December 2017
  • Dimensions: 15" W, 23" H
